Open Age Session - Does your doctor know your name, and does it matter?

29th March 2022

This session explores the healthcare geography of North West London and aims to understand the potential challenges and opportunities of greater integration between healthcare providers.

Comparing registered and resident populations in Primary Care Networks in England: an observational study

9th March 2022

Primary Care Networks (PCNs) are expected to be a building block for Integrated Care Systems but little is known about how they represent local communities. In this study we use publicly available data to identify how well PCNs footprints compare to where their patients live.

Evaluating the impact of a pulse oximetry remote monitoring programme on patients with Covid-19 in England

14th February 2022

Pulse oximetry played an important role in the monitoring of patients during the Covid-19 pandemic. In this project we evaluated the impact of the national Covid Oximetry @home program in England. Over the course of several separate studies we examined the benefit of the program at the population level and the individual level and also looked at which patient groups were more likely to receive the intervention.

Author: Dr Jonathan M. Clarke
