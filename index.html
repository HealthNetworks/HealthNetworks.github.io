Health Data Science


We are a team of clinicians and researchers affiliated with the Centre for Mathematics of Precision Healthcare and Patient Safety Translational Research Centre of Imperial College London.

Our work explores the notion of health systems as densely connected networks of patients and healthcare providers.

Using the latest techniques in data science and network analysis we have designed a series of tools to better understand how healthcare is provided, and importantly how these complex systems may be improved.
